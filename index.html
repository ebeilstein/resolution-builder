🗒️ Resolution Type — Optional Guidance
    1 Resolution Header
    Enter only the unique part of the title in ALL CAPS. The standard opening — "A RESOLUTION OF THE CITY COUNCIL, CITY OF LA VERNE, CALIFORNIA," — is added automatically.
    This is the file name used when the document downloads — "Resolution_" is added automatically (e.g. "Resolution_for_Retired_Annuitant_Robert_Russell.docx"). Not shown in the resolution document itself.
    2 Whereas Clauses
    Each clause starts with WHEREAS, (added automatically). All clauses except the last will end with "; and" — the last will end with a period. Add one clause per box.
    3 Resolution Sections
    Each section is labeled Section 1., Section 2., etc. automatically. Enter only the body text for each section. A final section is always added automatically — you don't need to write it: "The Mayor shall sign and the Deputy City Clerk shall certify to the passage and adoption of this Resolution and thereupon the same shall take effect and be in force."
